How to process a barako coffee in indigenous way

Ingredients List

Please prepare

  1. Dried bean coffee
  2. A Pan and spatula
  3. A coffee crusher or any tools that can crush the coffee beans.
  4. container for the coffee to be stored.

Procedures:

  1. Heat the pan for about 7 mins or until the pot is hot enough.
  2. Do not add any oil.
  3. place the dried beans of coffee into the hot pan and stirr it continually.
  4. Always stirr the beans untill the beans are toasted enough and or it's aroma is strong.
  5. After stirring for about 20 mins, Remove the beans for crushing.
  6. You can now enjoy a new and fresh coffee made from home!.

Please Be warned that drinking coffee in the night is not good if you want to sleep early and soundly, please be advised to use this only when you are in need to not sleep in an event like celebrations for this is very efective in not making you sleep in night time.
